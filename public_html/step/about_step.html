STEP INTRODUCTION STEP is Skill Training for Employbility Programme.
STEP was introduced on 3rd October 2015.
STEP is a committee which works with co-ordination of selected STEP students co-ordinators and STEP staff co-ordinator from each branch. STEP conducts various workshop/courses for students.
